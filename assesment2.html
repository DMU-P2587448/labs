Today we will talk about my favourite pizzas. Although, there are many types and styles of pizzas there are a few I like in particular.

The Toppings I Like On My Pizza

  • Ham
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Sweet Corn
  • Chicken
  • Chilli Peppers (in small quantity)

Why I Like These?

Some might think that my favourite toppings are bland, however I disagree. This is because these toppings have a lot of flavour when combined together in the right proportions. Also, it is important to know that I never have all of the above toppings on my pizza at once. This is because:

  1. Not all of the toppings complement each other
  2. It would be too much to have on your pizza, making it hard to consume
